3. Description of Service

GuardReel is a video editing application designed to help users view, edit, and export Tesla dashcam footage. The App provides:

  • Video Import: Import dashcam clips from USB drives or device storage
  • Multi-Camera Editing: Combine multiple camera angles into synchronized layouts
  • Telemetry Overlays: Display vehicle data (speed, GPS, etc.) from embedded metadata
