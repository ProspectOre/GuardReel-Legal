1. Introduction

GuardReel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application GuardReel (the "App").

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Video and Telemetry Data (Processed Locally)

The App processes Tesla dashcam video files that you import from USB drives or your device storage. These videos may contain embedded telemetry data including:

  • Vehicle speed
  • GPS coordinates and location data
  • Steering angle
  • Gear position
  • Autopilot/FSD status
  • Timestamps

Important: All video and telemetry processing occurs entirely on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your videos or telemetry data on any external servers. Your video content never leaves your device unless you choose to share or export it.

2.2 Analytics Data (Collected Automatically)

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App to help us understand usage patterns and improve our services:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Usage Data Features used, screens viewed, button taps Improve app functionality
Device Information Device model, iOS version, screen size Optimize compatibility
Performance Data Load times, export duration, memory usage Performance optimization
Crash Data Crash logs, error reports, stack traces Bug fixes and stability

This data is collected in an anonymized or pseudonymized form and cannot be used to directly identify you personally.

2.3 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to GuardReel Pro, Apple processes and stores all payment and subscription information. We receive only:

  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription (to remove ads)
  • Transaction verification: Cryptographic receipt data to validate your purchase

We do not receive or store your payment method, billing address, or Apple ID. All billing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.

2.4 Advertising Identifiers

If you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, we may collect:

  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA): Used to deliver personalized advertisements and measure ad effectiveness

If you deny tracking permission, we do not collect your IDFA and you will only see contextual (non-personalized) advertisements.

2.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you contact us)
  • Your Apple ID or account credentials
  • Your existing photos or videos (we only access files you explicitly import)
  • Your location in real-time (GPS data comes only from your Tesla video files)
  • Contacts, calendar, or other personal data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Process and edit your Tesla dashcam videos locally on your device
  • Display Advertisements: Show ads to support the free version of the App
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to add features and fix issues
  • Ensure Stability: Monitor crash reports to identify and resolve bugs
  • Optimize Performance: Measure and improve app speed and efficiency
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Respond to legal requests when required

4. Advertising

4.1 Ad-Supported Model

GuardReel is a free app supported by advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Ads help us keep the App free while continuing to develop new features. You can remove all advertisements and unlock all export qualities by subscribing to GuardReel Pro ($2.99/year). Pro subscribers do not see any ads, and no advertising data is collected for Pro users.

4.2 Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

The type of ads you see depends on your App Tracking Transparency choice:

Your Choice Ad Experience
Allow Tracking Personalized ads based on your interests and activity across apps. Ad partners may use your IDFA to measure ad effectiveness.
Ask App Not to Track Contextual ads that are not based on your personal data. You still see ads, but they are generic and not tailored to you.

4.3 Ad Partner Data Practices

Google AdMob may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. For more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from AdMob

4.4 Managing Ad Preferences

You can control personalized advertising through:

  • iOS Settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • In-App (EU Users): Settings → Ad Privacy Settings (for GDPR consent management)
  • Google Ad Settings: adssettings.google.com

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Firebase

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and diagnostic data

Firebase data is processed by Google. See the Firebase Privacy Policy.

5.2 Google AdMob

Displays advertisements and may collect advertising identifiers if permitted. See Section 4 for details.

5.3 Apple Frameworks

  • Photos Framework: Used only when you choose to save exported videos to your Photos library
  • MapKit: Used to display route maps in the dashboard overlay (map tiles loaded from Apple; your location is not shared)
  • App Tracking Transparency: Manages your tracking preferences

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

6.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Google (Firebase, AdMob) as described above, solely to provide app functionality and advertising
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the public
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you would be notified)

6.3 Your Content

We never access, view, or share the videos you import or create. Your content remains entirely under your control on your device.

7. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Local Processing: Video and telemetry data never leaves your device
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted to third-party services uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what is necessary for app functionality
  • No Account Required: We don't store personal credentials or account information

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 All Users

Regardless of your location, you can:

  • Control Tracking: Use iOS Settings to manage App Tracking Transparency
  • Delete Local Data: Remove all app data by deleting the App from your device
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Contact us to request deletion of your analytics data
  • Manage Ad Preferences: Adjust personalized ad settings through iOS or Google

9.2 European Economic Area (EEA) Residents — GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on:

  • Consent: For personalized advertising (via ATT and GDPR consent)
  • Legitimate Interest: For analytics and crash reporting to improve the App
  • Contract Performance: To provide the App's core video editing functionality

9.3 California Residents — CCPA/CPRA

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your personal information. If you deny ATT permission, we do not share your data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Categories of Personal Information Collected:

  • Identifiers (device identifiers, IDFA if permitted)
  • Internet/electronic network activity (app usage, crash data)
  • Geolocation data (only from Tesla video files you import, not real-time)

"Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information": We do not sell personal information. Denying ATT permission prevents sharing for targeted advertising.

9.4 Other Jurisdictions

If you are located in another jurisdiction with data protection laws (e.g., Brazil's LGPD, Canada's PIPEDA, UK GDPR), you may have similar rights. Please contact us to exercise your rights.
